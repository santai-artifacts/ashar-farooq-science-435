The Scientific Method

The simple loop behind every great discovery.

❓

1. Ask

Notice something and ask a clear question about it.

💡

2. Hypothesize

Make a testable guess at the answer.

🔬

3. Experiment

Run a fair test and collect the data.

📊

4. Conclude

See what the results say — then ask again.
